HOW IT STARTED

We Were Trekkers First

WanderHigh was born in the winter of 2023, somewhere between the snow-covered summit of Kedarkantha and the vast meadows of the Hampta Pass. Our founder, Nikhil Yadav, had just returned from his third Himalayan trek — and something had changed in him forever.

Trek after trek — from the wildflower valleys of the Valley of Flowers to the dramatic landscapes of Hampta Pass — Nikhil realized that every mountain had a story, and every trekker deserved to experience it safely, affordably, and responsibly. But most agencies were either too expensive, too careless, or too commercial.

That frustration became a mission. WanderHigh was built to be different — a trekking company that puts the trekker first. No shortcuts on safety, no compromise on experience, and always a deep respect for the mountains we walk through.

Since 2023, we've taken over 500 trekkers into the Himalayas — students, working professionals, solo travellers, and families. Every single one came back with a story worth telling.
