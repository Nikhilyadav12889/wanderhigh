HOW IT STARTED

We Were Trekkers First

WanderHigh was born on a cold January night at 3,800 metres, somewhere between Kedarkantha base camp and the summit. Our founder, Vikram Singh, had just led his tenth amateur group up a mountain — and realized that people weren't just looking for a trek. They were looking for a transformation.

Since 2018, we've taken over 2,000 people into the Himalayas — students, CEOs, grandparents, first-timers, and seasoned mountaineers. Every single one came back changed.
